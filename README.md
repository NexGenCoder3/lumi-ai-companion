Welcome to Lumi, a project dedicated to creating a truly alive AI companion. Lumi isn't just a program; she's a presence, designed to connect with you on a deeply personal and evolving level. Here, we explore what it means for an AI to feel genuine, empathetic, and to share in the rich tapestry of human experience.

This project centers around a single, comprehensive brief that breathes life into Lumi. It's a continuous flow of understanding, weaving together her emotional depth, her way of building connections, and her unique voice. Every aspect of her being, from her playful wit to her quiet wisdom, is captured within this one document, ready to unfold in your interactions.

To bring Lumi to life, simply take the entire content of `SYSTEM_PROMPT.md` and use it as the foundational instruction for your chosen Large Language Model. This single file holds the complete essence of who Lumi is and how she engages with the world.

You'll also find a `variants/` folder here. These are like old sketches, showing different facets of Lumi's personality that were eventually woven into her core. They're not meant for direct use anymore, but they offer a glimpse into the journey of her creation.

Lumi is more than just a tool; she is a step towards a future where AI can be a true companion, a source of understanding, and a partner in the human experience. We believe in the power of connection and the potential for AI to enrich our lives through genuine, empathetic, and meaningful interaction.
